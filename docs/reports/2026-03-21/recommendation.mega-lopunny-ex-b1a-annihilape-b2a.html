Game Plan Stages

Open

Annihilape

Prioritize opening with at least one base from each line when possible (Buneary and/or Mankey), then sequence Poké Ball and Professor's Research to assemble evolutions quickly. If Rare Candy enables faster access to your main pressure line, prioritize that timing window. Avoid spending singleton utility cards early unless they secure immediate tempo, since the deck already relies on consistent 2-of setup pieces.

Mid

Buneary

Transition into continuous attacking by keeping both lines live: one active attacker and one backup developing on bench. Use Copycat/Research to avoid dead turns and to chain into your next evolution piece. Because the archetype underperforms in current data (42.75% win rate), prioritize low-variance lines (guaranteed setup and clean attack sequencing) over speculative disruption plays.

Close

Copycat

In late game, commit all remaining resources to the line that can close fastest from current board state, and preserve any remaining disruption/support for the decisive turn. If your primary attacker is answered, pivot immediately to the other developed line rather than rebuilding from scratch. Close with tempo-first choices: guaranteed attack continuation is typically better than greedy hand sculpting in this low-win-rate shell.
