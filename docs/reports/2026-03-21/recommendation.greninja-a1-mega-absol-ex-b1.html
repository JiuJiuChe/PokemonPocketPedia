If Rare Candy and Greninja path are available, preserve sequencing to reach your Stage 2 timing quickly instead of overcommitting one-of utility cards early. Mid

Copycat

Transition from setup to pressure once Greninja or your main attacker line is online. Manage resources to maintain threat continuity: keep draw/search density high and avoid spending all utility in one turn cycle. Use the one-of dark package (Darkrai ex, Mega Absol ex) as pivot pressure when primary line is interrupted or when board state favors a different attacker profile. Close

Froakie

In closing turns, commit to whichever active threat has the clearest 2-turn path to finish prizes/board control, rather than splitting resources across multiple partial lines. Hold disruption/support (for example Cyrus or Red Card) for the highest-leverage turn where it protects your final attack sequence or disrupts opponent stabilization. If your primary line is weakened late, convert to Mega Absol ex/Darkrai ex as backup closer to preserve tempo.
