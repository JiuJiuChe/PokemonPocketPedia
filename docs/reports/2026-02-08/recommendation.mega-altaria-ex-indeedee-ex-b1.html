Game Plan

Mega Altaria ex Indeedee ex is a control-oriented Psychic deck that leverages Mega Altaria ex as the primary attacker while using Indeedee ex and Jirachi for support and disruption. The deck aims to establish board control through hand disruption (Cyrus, Mars, Sabrina) and defensive tools (Rocky Helmet) while building up Mega Altaria ex to sweep. With a 50.35% win rate and 3.43% meta share, this deck performs at a balanced level in the current meta, sitting at rank 7.

Game Plan Stages

Open

Mega Altaria ex

Prioritize finding Swablu in your opening hand or early turns using Poké Ball and Professor's Research. Ideally start with Swablu or Jirachi active. Use Professor's Research aggressively to dig for evolution pieces and key supporters. If you open with Indeedee ex, consider whether to promote it or pivot based on matchup. Avoid overcommitting resources early—this deck needs time to set up Mega Altaria ex. Use Copycat or Lisia to maintain hand size while searching for critical pieces. Chingling (if included) can serve as an alternative starter to buy time.

Mid

Professor's Research

Focus on evolving Swablu into Mega Altaria ex as your primary objective. Begin applying hand disruption with Cyrus and Mars to slow opponent's development while you build board state. Attach Rocky Helmet to your active attacker to accumulate chip damage. Use Sabrina strategically to eliminate low-HP bench threats or force unfavorable positioning. Manage your bench carefully—Jirachi and Indeedee ex should be positioned to either support or serve as backup attackers. Use Copycat to refill your hand after disruption plays. Assess whether to commit Indeedee ex as a secondary attacker or preserve it based on prize trade math.

Close

Swablu

Mega Altaria ex should be your primary closer, applying consistent damage while your disruption suite limits opponent's responses. Use Sabrina to secure KOs on damaged bench Pokémon or force out threats. Rocky Helmet chip damage can create favorable math for closing out games. If Mega Altaria ex is threatened, pivot to Indeedee ex as a backup attacker. Cyrus and Mars become critical in late game to prevent opponent from mounting comebacks. Manage your prize trade carefully—avoid giving up easy KOs on ex Pokémon unless it secures the win. Time your final disruption plays to deny opponent's win conditions.
