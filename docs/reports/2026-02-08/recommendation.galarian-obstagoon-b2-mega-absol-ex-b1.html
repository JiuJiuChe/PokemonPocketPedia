Game Plan

Galarian Obstagoon Mega Absol ex is a Dark-type control deck that leverages evolution acceleration via Rare Candy to establish board presence quickly. The deck aims to disrupt opponents with Cyrus, apply pressure with Mega Absol ex and Darkrai ex, and use Galarian Obstagoon as a tanky evolution line. Chingling provides early-game utility, while Piers ensures consistent Dark-type Pokémon search. The deck has a 44.53% win rate and 4.11% meta share, indicating it's a niche strategy requiring precise sequencing and matchup knowledge.

Game Plan Stages

Open

Chingling

Prioritize benching Galarian Zigzagoon and Chingling early using Poké Ball. Use Professor's Research aggressively to dig for Rare Candy and evolution pieces. Avoid overcommitting energy to Chingling unless necessary for stalling. Mulligan decisions should favor hands with Galarian Zigzagoon or search cards. If facing aggressive decks, consider benching Darkrai ex early to threaten counterattacks. Save Piers for mid-game unless you lack critical Dark-type Pokémon access.

Mid

Copycat

Use Rare Candy to evolve Galarian Zigzagoon directly into Galarian Obstagoon, bypassing Stage 1. Apply pressure with Darkrai ex while building energy on Mega Absol ex. Deploy Cyrus strategically to disrupt opponent's key turns (e.g., before they evolve or attack). Use Copycat to maintain hand size after Professor's Research. Attach Rocky Helmet to your active attacker if facing physical damage dealers. Piers becomes critical here to search missing Dark-type attackers or tech Pokémon. Manage bench space carefully—avoid clogging with unused Basics.

Close

Galarian Obstagoon

Transition to Mega Absol ex as your primary closer once sufficient energy is attached. Use Galarian Obstagoon to tank hits if Mega Absol ex is threatened. Cyrus can swing close games by limiting opponent's options during critical turns. Rocky Helmet chip damage may secure KOs on weakened targets. Conserve Copycat for late-game hand refresh if you've exhausted Professor's Research. If behind on prizes, prioritize high-value KOs with Mega Absol ex over spreading damage. Darkrai ex can clean up low-HP targets to close out games efficiently.

Tech Choices

  • Rocky Helmet (1x): Punishes contact-based attackers; strong against aggressive melee-focused decks.

  • Darkrai ex (1x): Provides secondary Dark-type pressure and type consistency with Piers.
