Meta Overview Summary

The meta remains highly stable week-over-week with Hydreigon Mega Absol ex maintaining dominance at #1 (2552 decks, 52.82% WR). Mega Altaria ex variants continue to proliferate across multiple top-10 positions, while Suicune ex Greninja ex holds steady at #2. The top deck distribution shows minimal volatility with only minor rank shuffling among established archetypes.

Current Highlights

  • Hydreigon Mega Absol ex leads with 2552 decks and 52.82% win rate, representing the most-played competitive deck
  • Mega Altaria ex dominates deck diversity with 4 distinct variants in top-10 (Chingling #3, Greninja #5, Gourgeist #6, Indeedee ex #8)
  • Staple cards show near-universal adoption: Professor's Research (99.3%), Rare Candy (99.2%), and archetype-specific 100% rates for Greninja and Mega Altaria ex lines

Changes vs Previous

  • Mega Altaria ex Greninja surged from rank #8 to #5 (+96 decks, 461→557) with improved 53.06% win rate
  • Galarian Obstagoon Mega Absol ex dropped from #7 to #10 despite slight win rate improvement (44.21%→45.1%), losing ground to Magnezone Oricorio which entered top-9
  • Lucky Ice Pop pick rate increased from 59.1% to 60.7%, while Rocky Helmet fell out of top-10 cards (was 65.1% pick rate)
