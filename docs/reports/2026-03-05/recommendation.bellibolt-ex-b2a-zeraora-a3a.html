Copycat

Deploy Bellibolt ex and Zeraora as dual attack strategy. Electric Generator ensures consistent energy for both Pokemon to attack. Use Copycat repeatedly for card advantage - this is the deck's primary engine. Cyrus disrupts opponent's hand while also drawing your cards.   • Not utilizing dual-attacker flexibility - both Bellibolt ex and Zeraora can attack; don't neglect one for the other
  • Failing to clear hand for Copycat - requires empty hand to maximize value; manage your draw efficiently
  • Cyrus timing - opponent also draws 8 cards per Pokemon; assess if opponent has enough Pokemon to benefit

  • Rocky Helmet passive defense - doesn't actively attack opponent; consider if offense would be better
