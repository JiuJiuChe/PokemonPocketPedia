Game Plan

Suicune ex Greninja ex is a #8 meta deck (127 samples, 44.1% WR, 2.55% share) that leverages a dual-ex strategy combining Suicune ex (Basic Water) with Greninja evolution line. Primary win condition: Suicune ex provides main damage output with Crystal Waltz (20 damage +20 per benched Pokemon, both players), while Greninja ex provides alternate attacker. Froakie→Greninja evolution line (Stage 2) complements the strategy. Irida heals 40 damage from Water-energy Pokemon. Cyrus and Mars provide disruption. Giant Cape provides survivability. The deck has below-median win rate (44.1%) compared to other meta decks, suggesting struggles against current aggressive meta despite strong engine cards.

Mid

Froakie

Primary attacker is Suicune ex with Greninja ex as alternate. Crystal Waltz (20 damage +20 per benched Pokemon, both players) deals massive early damage against spread-out decks. Copycat provides sustained card advantage if available. Cyrus disrupts opponent's hand while drawing your cards for more attacks. Irida heals 40 damage from Water-energy Pokemon for recovery between exchanges. Mars provides hand disruption. Giant Cape keeps Suicune ex/Greninja ex alive for extended battles. The dual-ex strategy allows flexibility - attack with Suicune ex when opponent vulnerable, use Greninja ex for alternate damage. Indeedee ex likely provides search/healing/utility support. When opponent has cards in hand, Mars provides disruption. The Water-type synergy with Irida healing enables sustained pressure. If opponent plays aggressive decks, Irida healing allows recovery.

Close

Greninja

Irida healing enables final attacks by recovering damage. Mars disrupts opponent's hand for final push. Indeedee ex provides support/utility for finishing. Common Pitfalls

  • Cyrus self-draw - opponent draws 8 cards per Pokemon in play; assess board state before playing Cyrus. Can backfire if opponent has many Pokemon.
  • Rare Candy fragility - 2x Rare Candy essential but disruption leaves no Stage 1 fallback Pokemon; Froakie→Greninja evolution disrupted leaves no backup.
  • Suicune ex Crystal Waltz opponent damage - Both players take damage from benched Pokemon, can hurt you too if spread out.
  • Irida timing - Requires Water energy attached to Pokemon; if mana management fails, healing inaccessible.
  • Evolution fragility - Froakie→Greninja→Greninja ex requires multiple turns; opponent may kill before evolution completes.
  • Dual-ex vulnerability - 2x Suicune ex + 1x Greninja ex means 3x potential disruption targets; if ex Pokemon disrupted, loses win condition.
  • Mars late game dependency - Only effective if opponent has significant hand cards; weak against empty-handed opponents.
  • Indeedee ex role uncertainty - 100% presence but ability/text unknown; critical support Pokemon unclear utility.
  • Guzma without Tool context - 66.7% presence but opponent may not have Tools; wasted turn if so.
  • Mesagoza minimal - 33.3% presence suggests situational utility rather than core support.
  • Evolution timing without Stage 1 - 100% Rare Candy but no Stage 1 Pokemon; if Rare Candy disrupted, no evolution fallback.
