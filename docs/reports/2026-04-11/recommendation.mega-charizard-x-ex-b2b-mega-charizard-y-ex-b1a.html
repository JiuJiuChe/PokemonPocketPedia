Flame Patch

Commit to the mega line that gives the shortest, safest finish from current board state, then protect that line with remaining utility (Sabrina, Pokémon Center Lady, Rocky Helmet, or disruption supporters as available). Preserve at least one hand-quality card if possible so the final two turns are not topdeck-dependent. If the first mega push is answered, close with Entei ex pressure plus remaining support rather than rebuilding an entirely new chain.
