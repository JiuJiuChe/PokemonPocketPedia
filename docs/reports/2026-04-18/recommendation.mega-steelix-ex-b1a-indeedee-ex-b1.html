Game Plan

This deck is a durability-oriented steel/psychic midrange shell centered on Mega Steelix ex plus Indeedee ex, with heavy supporter consistency and multiple sustain tools. Core structure is highly fixed in samples: Brock, Indeedee ex, Mega Steelix ex, Onix, Poké Ball, Pokémon Center Lady, and Professor's Research are all avg_count 2.0 with presence_rate 1.0, while Heavy Helmet and Lucky Ice Pop are frequent secondary durability slots (avg_count 1.6667, presence_rate 1.0). In ranked play, the plan is to establish Mega Steelix ex pressure early, maintain board through healing/tool value, and grind out tempo advantages with efficient supporter timing.

Game Plan Stages

Open

Brock

Prioritize opening with Onix or Indeedee ex plus one consistency card (Poké Ball or Professor's Research). In early turns, build the Mega Steelix ex line while preserving at least one backup threat on board to avoid collapsing to disruption. Use Brock/Research sequencing to secure stable development before spending reactive singleton cards. If sustain cards appear early, hold them until they convert into an extra effective attack turn rather than using them immediately.

Mid

Indeedee ex

Transition into a board-control trade pattern: pressure with Mega Steelix ex while rotating to Indeedee ex as needed to maintain attacking continuity. Time Heavy Helmet, Lucky Ice Pop, and Pokémon Center Lady for high-leverage turns where they deny key knockouts or force unfavorable opponent exchanges. Use Copycat/Cyrus to swing tempo when the opponent is about to stabilize. Because win rate is near-even (50.49%), cleaner sequencing and durability timing are likely the biggest edge drivers.

Close

Mega Steelix ex

In closing turns, commit resources to the attacker that offers the safest 2-turn finish path and preserve one disruption/heal effect for the decisive exchange. If Mega Steelix ex is checked, pivot to Indeedee ex pressure rather than rebuilding from zero. Prioritize guaranteed attacks and survivability over greedy hand sculpting once the prize race tightens.
