Game Plan

This archetype is an electric tempo deck centered on Mega Manectric ex plus Zeraora, backed by high consistency and tool/item pressure. Core cards are very stable in samples: Mega Manectric ex, Zeraora, Poké Ball, and Professor's Research are all avg_count 2.0 with presence_rate 1.0, while Rocky Helmet, Copycat, and Electric Generator are frequent support pieces (avg_count 1.6667/1.3333/1.3333; all presence_rate 1.0). Ranked plan is to establish early board and momentum, convert into continuous Mega Manectric ex pressure, then use utility supporters/tools to keep initiative and close efficiently.

Game Plan Stages

Open

Mega Manectric ex

Prioritize opening hands that contain at least one core attacker line piece (Zeraora or Electrike/Mega Manectric access) plus a consistency card (Poké Ball or Professor's Research). Sequence search and draw first to secure the next two turns of pressure rather than spending singleton interaction cards immediately. If both lines are available, develop the line with the fastest guaranteed attack while staging the other as backup to preserve tempo after disruption.

Mid

Poké Ball

Midgame should focus on uninterrupted attacking: transition into Mega Manectric ex pressure while Zeraora or secondary pieces maintain board threat density. Use Copycat and Research to keep hand quality high enough for consecutive productive turns. Deploy Electric Generator and tool/support cards on turns that create measurable tempo or survivability gains, not for low-impact value. Because win rate is strong for its share (52.26% at 4.27% share), maintain disciplined sequencing and avoid unnecessary high-variance plays.

Close

Professor's Research

In closing turns, commit to the attacker line that gives the shortest and safest finish path from current board state. Preserve one interaction piece (for example Cyrus, Sabrina if present, or survivability tool timing) for the opponent’s stabilization attempt. If the primary attacker is answered, pivot immediately to the prepared secondary threat instead of rebuilding from scratch, prioritizing attack continuity over hand perfection.
